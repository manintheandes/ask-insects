# Insects Deep Research

## What To Say

To use it, just ask Claude Code or Codex:

```text
Use Ask Insects Deep Research to investigate how Aedes aegypti olfactory receptors connect to host seeking, and bring back a cited report from source records.
```

That is enough. Ask Insects gathers what it needs and turns the work into a research memo.

## What It Does

Insects Deep Research is for work that needs a longer answer than a normal Ask Insects question. It can:

- investigate a broad question over multiple steps;
- search public scientific, biological, ecological, public-health, and technical context;
- compare source lanes against each other;
- synthesize papers, observation records, images, videos, genomes, behavior datasets, and atlas records;
- produce a longer memo with citations;
- follow a requested structure, such as sections, tables, or JSON;
- name source gaps instead of smoothing over missing evidence.
