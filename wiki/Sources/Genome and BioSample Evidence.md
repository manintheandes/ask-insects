# Genome and BioSample Evidence

Source IDs: `ncbi_datasets_genome`, `ncbi_biosamples`, `vectorbase_aedes_genomics`, `bold_api`

This page covers Aedes aegypti genomes, genes, transcripts, proteins, genome features, BioSamples, and DNA barcode evidence.

What it contains:

- 19,241 hosted gene records
- 33,961 hosted transcript records
- 28,317 hosted protein records
- 12,871 hosted genome-feature records
- 20,656 hosted BioSample records
- 3,561 hosted DNA barcode records
- NCBI assembly and package metadata
- VectorBase/VEuPathDB GFF, annotated protein FASTA, and GO records
- BioSample strain, isolate, collection date, geography, tissue, isolation source, organization, and linked SRA metadata when exposed
