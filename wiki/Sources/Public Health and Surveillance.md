# Public Health and Surveillance

Source IDs: `aedes_public_health_guidance`, `aedes_paho_dengue_surveillance`, `aedes_who_dengue_surveillance`, `aedes_cdc_dengue_surveillance`, `aedes_ncvbdc_dengue_surveillance`, `aedes_opendatasus_dengue_surveillance`, `aedes_wolbachia_interventions`, `vectornet_aedes_surveillance`, `aedes_literature_facets`

This page covers official Aedes aegypti public-health guidance, dengue surveillance report evidence, VectorNet surveillance rows, Wolbachia intervention evidence, and literature-derived public-health records.

What it contains:

- 31,964 hosted public-health records
- WHO, PAHO, CDC, India NCVBDC, Brazil OpenDataSUS, and ECDC guidance or surveillance pages
- 18,587 CDC dengue surveillance records
- 221 India NCVBDC dengue surveillance records
- 2,177 Brazil OpenDataSUS SINAN dengue aggregate records from 1,956,578 source CSV rows
- 538 PAHO dengue surveillance records
- 25 WHO dengue surveillance records
- 563 VectorNet Aedes surveillance records
- dengue, Zika, Aedes life-cycle, vector-control, travel-medicine, Wolbachia, community-prevention, and species-factsheet evidence
- PAHO dengue situation report HTML parsed at report, subregion, serotype, figure, and table grain
- CDC ArboNET pages, visualization JSON configs, linked CSV rows, and limitation evidence
- India NCVBDC national and state/UT annual dengue cases/deaths, including the latest-two-complete-year summary
- Brazil OpenDataSUS country-year, state-year, country-week, and residence-state-week SINAN dengue aggregates
- WHO dengue pages, WER updates, WPRO situation updates, archive links, and dashboard locators
- source gaps for PAHO/PLISA country-week dashboard rows until stable machine-readable access or authorized access exists
