# Ask Insects Obsidian Vault Design

## Goal

Create an Ask Insects Obsidian vault that follows the Ask Monarch and Ask Just public wiki shape as closely as possible, while replacing company-specific content with Ask Insects source-plane content.

## Shape

The vault lives at `wiki/` in the Ask Insects repo and is exposed as `/Users/josh/Documents/Ask Insects Wiki`, matching the local access pattern used by Ask Just and Ask Monarch.

The page family is:

- `Ask Insects.md`
- `Source Map.md`
- `Ask Insects Updates.md`
- `Guides/Team Setup.md`
- `Guides/Question Cookbook.md`
- `Guides/Insects Deep Research.md`
- `Guides/Insects Skills.md`
- `Sources/*.md`

## Rules

- Do not reuse Ask Monarch or Ask Just publish site IDs.
- Keep source claims backed by the Ask Insects hosted health and source map.
- Keep known gaps visible.
- Keep Obsidian Publish enabled in the vault config so the site can be connected later.
