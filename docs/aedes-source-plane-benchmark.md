# Aedes Source Plane Benchmark

Ask Insects is building toward a very deep practical `Aedes aegypti` intelligence source plane, but the repo should not claim the world-largest or world-deepest title yet.

The current hosted source plane is broad and integrated: the latest hosted health proof records `1,463,778` records across genomics, literature, observations, media, neurobiology, traits, abundance, resistance, vector competence, ecology, public-health, taxonomy, climate-source, occurrence-compendium, population-genomics, and WHO resistance-guidance lanes. It includes `872,001` VectorBase/VEuPathDB Aedes genomics records, `46,252` Aedes video-atom records, `7,160` VectorByte VecDyn abundance records, `5,828` verified bounded Aedes image-byte mirrors with checksum, byte size, dimensions, and raw asset paths. The public-health lane includes `221` official India NCVBDC dengue cases/deaths records at state/UT-year, national-year, and latest-two-complete-year summary grain plus `29,402` official Brazil OpenDataSUS SINAN dengue aggregate records from `30,767,966` source CSV rows. The ecology lane includes `500` bounded WorldClim 10-minute bioclim raster samples joined to global Aedes occurrence-compendium coordinates and `993` bounded WorldClim climate samples joined directly to coordinate-bearing GBIF, iNaturalist, and Mosquito Alert observation rows. That proves a strong integrated query plane. It does not prove that Ask Insects is larger or deeper than every source-specific system in the world.

The machine-readable benchmark lives at `config/aedes-source-plane-benchmark.json`.

## What This Proves

Ask Insects currently has a provenance-backed, atomically queryable Aedes source plane that unifies many lanes researchers normally inspect separately.

It has a stronger cross-lane answer surface than any one source-specific upstream system listed here, because it can join records from genomics, public observations, abundance samples, literature, videos, still images, neurobiology, public health, resistance, vector competence, and traits.

## What This Does Not Prove

This does not prove Ask Insects is the largest Aedes database in the world.

This does not prove Ask Insects is deeper than VectorBase for VectorBase-native genomics, deeper than GBIF for GBIF-native occurrences, deeper than IR Mapper for IR Mapper-native resistance maps, or deeper than VectorByte for VectorByte-native trait and abundance curation.

Those upstream systems remain source-specific leaders in their own lanes.

## Claim Ladder

Safe now:

Ask Insects is a broad, integrated, provenance-backed `Aedes aegypti` query plane.

Strong, but should be refreshed before public use:

Ask Insects is one of the most comprehensive integrated public `Aedes aegypti` intelligence planes.

Not allowed yet, because it is not proven:

Ask Insects is the deepest, largest, or most extensive `Aedes aegypti` intelligence source in the world.

## Comparator Table

| Source | What It Leads In | Ask Insects Position | Gap |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| VectorBase / VEuPathDB | Official vector genomics, annotations, genome browser, identifiers, and analysis tools. | Ask Insects normalizes VectorBase files into the same query surface as observations, traits, media, public health, and literature. | Not a full replacement for every VectorBase web tool, browser track, workflow, or future release. |
| NCBI Entrez / Datasets / BioSample / SRA / PubMed / PMC | Primary biological identifiers, samples, sequence records, run metadata, publications, and open full text. | Ask Insects narrows and connects Aedes-relevant NCBI records across multiple lanes. | Not a full mirror of all NCBI databases, raw reads, Gene, Protein, PubMed, or PMC. |
| GBIF | Global biodiversity occurrence aggregation and taxonomy. | Ask Insects stores a bounded Aedes GBIF slice with raw payloads and joins it to other observation and ecology lanes. | Not a complete live mirror of every GBIF occurrence, dataset version, or taxonomic update. |
| iNaturalist | Public community observations, photos, quality grades, and annotations. | Ask Insects turns a bounded Aedes observation/photo slice into queryable observations, media, payloads, and image atoms. | Not a live mirror of all comments, identifications, annotations, projects, or future observations. |
| Mosquito Alert | Expert-reviewed citizen-science mosquito reports and images. | Ask Insects ingests the Aedes slice through GBIF/IPT and joins it to the wider source plane. | Not a replacement for the app, expert-review workflow, or BioImage Archive chunks. |
| VectorNet | Validated European and neighboring-region vector surveillance. | Ask Insects indexes the Aedes Darwin Core Archive slice and ecology summaries. | Not a substitute for the expert network, maps, unpublished validation, or future releases. |
| BOLD | Public DNA barcode specimen, sequence, and trace-file retrieval. | Ask Insects stores Aedes barcode rows beside genomics and other source lanes. | Not a mirror of every package, trace file, private record, or BIN workflow. |
| IR Mapper | Open-access insecticide-resistance maps, filters, and standardized susceptibility records. | Ask Insects indexes Aedes resistance rows and combines them with literature-derived marker evidence. | Not a replacement for maps, monthly updates, model layers, or user uploads. |
| VectorByte / VecTraits / VecDyn | Vector trait and abundance data curation. | Ask Insects normalizes Aedes VecTraits rows into trait atoms and a bounded VecDyn dataset into sample-level abundance/count atoms with raw JSON provenance. | Current coverage is a bounded trait slice plus a bounded VecDyn receipt, not all VectorByte species, all VecDyn abundance data, or tools. |
| OpenAlex / PubMed / PMC Open Access | Literature discovery, biomedical identifiers, and legal open full text. | Ask Insects turns material Aedes literature into queryable papers, full-text chunks, media, and derived candidate facts. | Not a full mirror of all records, PDFs, supplements, image tables, or claims. |
| WHO / PAHO / CDC public health | Official public-health surveillance and guidance context. | Ask Insects keeps Aedes-relevant disease, guidance, surveillance reports, dashboard locators, and intervention context beside vector records. | Public-health rows are human disease and guidance context, not mosquito occurrence records; dashboard locator records do not imply row-queryable dashboard cells. |

## Benchmark Rule

Before using a world-largest or world-deepest claim, update the machine-readable benchmark with fresh hosted proof and a refreshed comparator inventory. If an upstream total is unknown, the benchmark must say unknown rather than infer completeness.
